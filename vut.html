'R E A P   /   S O W'

Valour Up Twice
Album Cover & Package (2015)

The 3D modeled deers on the cover of 'Reap / Sow' show the more modern feel on the new album of Valour Up Twice then the previous album 'Kill Your Darlings'. Their songs which can be gently and mystique can end in heavy outbursts. This can also be found in the characteristics of deers (mystique and beautifull but dangerous and agressive). You can see these two elements in the movement of the deers too. They can either be dancing or fighting.

(Cinema 4d, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Screenprint, Lasercutter)
