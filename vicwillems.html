'N I K S   O M   J E   V O O R   T E   S C H A M E N;

Vic Willems
Artwork(2015)

'Niks om je voor te schamen' (or: 'Nothing to be ashamed of') is the first project of Vic Willems. Vic is a singer-songwriter who tells brave stories from a fragile perspective. For one of the songs ('Zijwieltjes') I made a artwork based on the lyrics.

(Photoshop / Illustrator)
