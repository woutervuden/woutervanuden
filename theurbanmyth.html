Valour Up Twice
Videoclip (2015)

This videoclip for Indie / Alternative Rock band Valour Up Twice combines the feel of a traditional videoclip with a studiosession. The main aim of Valour Up Twice is to make music which can for loved for it's honesty, energy and quality. The concept was inspired by the Woodstock sessions which can be seen in the use of splitscreens and reflected images.
